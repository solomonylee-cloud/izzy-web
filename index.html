Good to know

Questions, answered.

How do I talk to Izzy?

On Base, tap Izzy and ask — one tap, one question, then it's quiet until your next tap. On Pro, just talk: no tapping, Izzy stays awake and answers between questions. No wake word, no menus, gloves stay on.

What does Izzy know?

Izzy is built for the mountain — live conditions, trail and lift status, grooming, resort info, where to grab a bite, and turn-by-turn help getting around. On Pro and Family, Izzy goes further with live web search, so you can ask about current events, nearby spots, and just about anything beyond the slopes.

How does fall detection work?

Pro watches for a hard impact followed by 30 seconds of stillness, then auto-alerts ski patrol with your last GPS — unless you tap "I'm OK" first. Sensitivity is adjustable on the Safety tab.

Can I call for help with my voice?

Yes, on Pro — just say "Izzy, I need ski patrol." Izzy texts your emergency contact your live GPS and offers to call patrol, hands on your poles. Base gets a one-tap SOS button.

How does Kid SOS work?

Kids fire SOS from a tap, their voice, or a lock-screen widget. Every parent gets a critical-alert push that breaks through silent mode with the kid's live GPS, and patrol is called in parallel — parents acknowledge so the kid sees "Mom's on the way."

What's the difference between Pro and Family?

Pro covers one rider. Family covers up to 6 (2 adults + 4 kids), gives everyone full Pro, and adds the family stack — live Find My Family map, family chat, Kid SOS, end-of-day recap, and COPPA kid mode for under-13s. Solo rider → Pro; household → Family. See the plans →
